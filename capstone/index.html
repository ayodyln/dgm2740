Dragon's Keep

The Dragon's Keep has been serving Utah Valley for generations. We feel treated every time we have a parent bring their children to share the memories they’ve made at The Dragon’s Keep. It continues to be our mission to deliver the most convenient location for you to come, sit, and enjoy your favorite hobbies with a knowledgeable staff who share the same passion for the wonderful product we get stock. What has always made Dragon’s Keep the greatest place to work? You - The customer. Thank you for 30+ amazing years and we look forward to seeing you in one of our now four locations throughout the Utah Valley.

What we offer:

Comics

We are proud to proclaim that we are the only comic book shop in Utah Valley. We carry the greatest comic hits, ranging from the awesome, adventure packed Marvel Comics to some of the amazing art filled image comics. We carry Marvel, DC, Image, IDW, Dark Horse, and many more fantastic small publishers that have amazing storylines as well as some of the most stunning artwork that has been produced by modern artists. Contact us through the contact page if you're an artist and want to do a signing at one of our stores, as well as if you want to sell some of your artwork as well.

Miniature Gaming

Warhammer 40K and Age of Sigmar are Dragon's Keeps Staples when it comes to war gaming miniatures. We have escalation leagues that are 6 weeks long for Warhammer that have a tournament at the end. The escalation leagues are all store inclusive. We have paint nights at most of the stores, as well as painting lessons given by SD paint studios. SD paint studios is a partner with Dragon’s Keep that will do commissions for any miniature models. Ask a Dragon’s Keep Team Member or fill out a contact form with any and all inquiries about commissions.

Trading Card Games

Trading Card Games are the lifeblood of Dragon’s Keep. We hold major events for card games including but not limited to Magic The Gathering, Pokemon, YuGiOh!, and Dragon Ball Super, plus many more! Contact your local Dragon’s Keep and ask one of our team members about new events or visit the calendar of your specific store in order to find out when events are happening. We also do pre-orders for booster boxes of Magic, Dragon Ball, and Pokemon! We sell Pokemon, Magic, and YuGiOh! singles, and take TCG trade ins for 50% of their TCG Player Market Value.

Board Games

Board games are a great way for families to bond during family game night! We carry a wide variety of different board games from card games like Smash Up and Fluxx, to adventurous dungeon crawlers like Board Game Geek's #1 rated game Gloomhaven and Betrayal House on the Hill. We also host board game leagues at most of our locations! The prizes include store credit, and much more! If your interested in joining one of our board game leagues, contact the store closest to you and we can sign you up! If you come to any of our stores we have used board games for you to try out for free, and if you can't stay too long to play, you can rent them for a small fee!

Role-Playing Games

Here at the Dragon's Keep, RPGs are one of the activities we are most known for! Most of our stores have a variety of Dice, Books, Mats as well as miniatures for purchase. Our most played game is D&D 5th edition, but we also run games of Pathfinder, Star Wars, Call of Cthulu, and many others! Call your local Dragons Keep, or go to the calendar to find when your local RPG night is. We also are looking for more DMs at the Provo and Orem store to help us run our RPG night! If you're interested, let a Dragons Keep team member know!
